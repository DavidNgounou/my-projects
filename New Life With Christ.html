Introduction into your new step with God.

It is important to make God the first in everything you do this is because he is the one who is the owner of your life, he is the one who created you, he sent his son Jesus to die on the cross for you, Though he sacrificed his only son he asks us in change our heart. When talking about the heart i am talking about the our hope, our trust, our love. That is what God wants from us. In a certain verse in the Bible in the book of Revelation God says "he stands at the door and knock if anyone here my voice and opens the door I will dine with him and him with me".

In order to accept Jesus-Christ as your Lord and personal Saviour? It is important to follow The steps below.

  1. You need to accept that Jesus died on the cross to set you free.
  2. You need to recognize that you have sinned against God.
  3. You need to ask for forgiveness for all the sins you have committed.
  4. You have to ask God to enter in your life.
