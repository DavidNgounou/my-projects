Aim: TO PREPARE NITROGEN DIOXIDE

Requirements: crystals of lead(II) nitrate, test tube or boiling tube, U-tube, freezing mixture bath;

Procedure:

  1. place a few crystals of lead (II) nitrate in the boiling tube and heat strongly.
  2. It crackle, melt and decompose to yield solid residue of lead (II) oxide, oxygen gas and brown fumes of nitrogen dioxide gas.
  3. The residue of lead (II) oxide is left in the boiling tube and the gases evolved are passed through a U-tube immersed in a freezing mixture.
  4. The brown gas nitrogen dioxide is liquefied in the U-tube while oxygen escape as a gas.

2Pb(NO3)2 (s) -------------> 2PbO2 (s) + 4NO2 (g) + O2 (g)
